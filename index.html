Permacommons

Minding the Future

Permacommons is building a permanent home for useful shared resources that are maintained with AI assistance. We are starting with open source software.

Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where the amount of human labor scales with the quality of AI systemsâ€”as they improve, human effort decreases, but never at the expense of quality, usability, or security.

Our long-term goal is to sustain compute and human effort through an endowment fund, making the commons truly permanent. But we're taking it one step at a time, starting by building a community that welcomes the responsible use of AI in open source development.

Currently, the best place to follow our work is on GitHub. We are focusing on simple tools and scripts and will work our way up as the technology improves.

Our Vision

To forge possibility into abundance for all humanity.
